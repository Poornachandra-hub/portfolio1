How I Work

THE PROCESS

01
Brief & Discovery
We start with a conversation — understanding your vision, audience, and the feeling you want the video to leave behind. This shapes every decision that follows.
02
Assembly & Story Edit
Raw footage gets organized and shaped into a narrative. I build the rhythm and pacing in this stage — the backbone of a great edit.
03
Sound & Motion
Music, SFX, motion graphics, and transitions are woven in — transforming the assembly into something you can feel in your chest.
04
Color Grade & Delivery
A cinematic grade is applied to set the mood and tone. Final files delivered in your required specs — on time, every time.
